ZenShift Features

Built to match the live app: Bedrock-powered AI coach, curated catalog, on-demand generation pipeline, and offline-aware sync. All content here maps to shipped code.

AI Coach Conversations

Talk naturally about stress, sleep, or focus. Bedrock Nova Micro with LangGraph state picks the right session and offers custom generation when confidence is low. Safety chains keep guidance in-bounds.

Custom Session Generation

Approve a custom session and the backend generates script → TTS narration → mixes ambient music in minutes, returning signed URLs to the app. Moderation runs before generation starts.

Smart Library Matching

Discover 20 curated sessions across 10 categories (Stress, Focus, Sleep, Energy, Travel, and more). AI matches duration, mood, and intent for quick relief.

Progress Tracking & Learning

History, streaks, and favorites with personal notes sync across devices with offline-first storage. Relief ratings and weekly goals keep you accountable.

How Your AI Coach Helps You Find Calm

Need → AI coach → match or generate. Fast path to relief, grounded in the real catalog and generation pipeline.

1

Express Your Need Conversationally

Say what's happening and how much time you have (3–30 minutes). Need-based home chips and chat capture stressor, goal, and duration.

2

AI Analyzes and Matches

Library matcher scores your intent against 20 curated sessions. If confidence is high, you get an instant recommendation. If not, the coach offers custom generation.

3

Choose Your Path to Relief

Play instantly or approve custom generation. The backend writes the script, synthesizes narration, mixes music, and returns signed URLs—typically within a few minutes.

4

Let AI Learn Your Patterns

History, favorites, and relief ratings sync with offline-first storage. Streaks and weekly goals keep you consistent while recommendations improve over time.

Who It's For: Busy professionals, beginners seeking guided structure, experienced meditators looking for variety, anyone wanting to build consistent mindfulness habits.

About One Ping Fan Venture LLC

One Ping Fan Venture LLC builds ZenShift on a fully serverless AWS stack (AppSync, Cognito, DynamoDB, S3/CloudFront, SQS). Bedrock Nova Micro powers the AI coach; a separate generation worker creates narrated audio with background music.

Our Mission

Deliver fast, contextual relief for busy people using responsible AI and reliable cloud infrastructure.

Our Technology

LangChain + Bedrock for conversations, library matcher for recommendations, and an SQS-backed generation pipeline for custom audio. Offline-aware mobile client built with Expo and Amplify GraphQL.
