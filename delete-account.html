ZenShift Meditation Coach respects your privacy and your right to delete your personal data. This page explains how to delete your account and all associated data from the app. Whether you choose to delete your account through the app or need additional assistance, we've outlined everything you need to know.

1. Overview

When you delete your account in ZenShift Meditation Coach, all data associated with your account is permanently removed. The app is designed to be privacy-first, with most data stored locally on your device. Deleting your account ensures your personal information is completely removed from our systems.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you've backed up any information you want to keep before proceeding.

2. Delete Account In-App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly through the ZenShift Meditation Coach mobile app:

Android (Google Play)

Step 1: Open the ZenShift Meditation Coach app

Step 2: Navigate to your Profile (bottom right icon)

Step 3: Look for "Delete Account" or "Privacy Settings"

Step 4: Confirm you want to delete your account and all associated data

Step 5: Your account and data are permanently deleted immediately

iOS (Coming Soon)

The iOS version of ZenShift Meditation Coach is coming soon. When available, the account deletion process will be identical to the Android version. Check back for updates.

No Login Required

ZenShift is designed to work offline with minimal account authentication. You can delete your account directly from the app without needing to log in first.

3. What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information is permanently removed:

  • Session History: All meditation session records, including timestamps, duration, and completion status
  • User Preferences: Your meditation preferences, favorite categories, difficulty levels, and app settings
  • Progress Data: Your stats, streaks, achievements, and weekly goals
  • Favorites: All marked favorite sessions and saved content
  • Personalization Data: Your expressed needs, preferences, and recommendation signals
  • Account Information: Any account-level data associated with your profile
  • Experiment Data: Data related to A/B tests or experiments you participated in

4. What Data Is Retained

No data is retained after account deletion. All personal and usage information is permanently removed from our systems and your device. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

The ZenShift app stores data locally on your device using encrypted storage. When you delete your account through the app, this local data is wiped from your device's storage.

5. Immediate Deletion

Your data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. There is no waiting period or processing delay.

When you confirm account deletion in the app:

  • All data is instantly removed from your device's local storage
  • Your account is immediately deactivated
  • No further data is retained or processed

This instant deletion reflects our privacy-first design philosophy. Because the app primarily stores data locally on your device rather than on remote servers, deletion is immediate and complete.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deleting it?

No, account deletion is permanent. Once you delete your account and data, it cannot be recovered. If you wish to use ZenShift again, you can reinstall the app and create a new account.

Will my data be deleted from my device?

Yes, all local data stored on your device will be deleted when you use the in-app deletion feature. If you prefer to manually delete the app data, you can uninstall the app from your device, which will also remove all locally stored information.

Is there a way to delete my account without using the app?

The easiest method is to use the in-app deletion feature. If you no longer have access to the app, you can contact our support team at zenshift-support@onepingfanventure.com to request account deletion. Please provide any identifying information that will help us locate your account.

Does ZenShift store my data on its servers?

No. ZenShift is designed as a privacy-first app. All your meditation data, preferences, and activity are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do not maintain a central database of your personal information unless you explicitly choose to sync or back up your data.

How do I uninstall the ZenShift app?

Android: Press and hold the ZenShift app icon on your home screen, then select "Uninstall" or go to Settings > Apps > ZenShift > Uninstall.
iOS: Press and hold the ZenShift app icon, select "Remove App," then confirm deletion.

What if I accidentally deleted my account?

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You would need to reinstall the app and create a new account. If you believe there was an error, contact zenshift-support@onepingfanventure.com for assistance.

Does ZenShift comply with data deletion laws?

Yes. ZenShift complies with data protection regulations including the GDPR (European Union), CCPA (California), and other applicable privacy laws. Our immediate deletion process ensures compliance with the "right to be forgotten" provisions in these regulations.

What about crash reports or analytics?

Any anonymized crash reports or analytics data are also deleted as part of the account deletion process. We do not retain any identifiable information that could be linked to your deleted account.

How do I verify my account was deleted?

After deleting your account through the app, you'll receive immediate confirmation. If you need additional verification, contact our support team with your deletion request date, and we can confirm the deletion status.
